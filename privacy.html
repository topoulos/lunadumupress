Last updated: March 4, 2026

Luna Dumu Press (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Technical data: Like most websites, our hosting and service providers may automatically receive basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and approximate location. This is typically used for security, reliability, and performance.

How We Use Your Information

Email Subscriptions

If you subscribe, you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in the footer of any email we send. Unsubscribing stops future emails.

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies from third-party services (for example, embedded forms). We also store your “spoiler filter” preference using your browser’s local storage so your selection is remembered on future visits. You can clear this by clearing your browser’s site data.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to help operate our site. These services may collect or process data according to their own privacy policies:

Analytics

We may add privacy-friendly analytics in the future to understand traffic and improve the site. If we do, this policy will be updated.

Data Retention

We keep subscriber information for as long as you remain subscribed or as needed to operate the mailing list. If you unsubscribe, your email may be removed or suppressed according to our email provider’s standard practices.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. The simplest way to stop emails is to unsubscribe using the link in any email.
